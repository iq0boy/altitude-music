Neo walked into the studio with a clear cinematic vision: turning his intimate stories and everyday life into a true sonic feature film. That's how "MOVIE 2K25" was born — a three-track EP that kicks off his Hollywood season. A summer project, raw and sincere, produced from A to Z within the walls of Altitude Music.

## Art direction

The whole challenge of this project was to convey, both visually and musically, the mood of Neo's relationships. To translate that cinematic facet, we worked on immersive sonic textures that tie the tracks together. The goal was clear: make the listener feel instantly dropped into the scene from the very first second.

## The tracklist in detail

The project revolves around a trilogy of strong tracks that tell the summer chapter of this long movie. We fine-tuned the coherence of the tracklist to create a smooth progression:

- **FILM**: the ideal intro that lays the foundations of the concept and of Neo's universe.
- **BONNE COMPAGNIE**: a track at the heart of summer, blending raw emotion and polished arrangements.
- **RONNIE**: the song that closes this first chapter with striking artistic sincerity.

## What changed everything

To give the vocals that "big screen" color, we put the emphasis on the clarity of Neo's intimate stories while keeping a modern, punchy edge. Loudness and dynamics management across the three tracks lets the EP sound ultra-consistent, ready to land straight into the artist's Spotify and YouTube playlists the day it drops.
