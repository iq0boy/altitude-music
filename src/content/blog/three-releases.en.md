March was a strong month for music in LLN. Here are three releases worth your time.

## NAWAH — Skyline

The single ahead of the EP. Production by Iliès, mixed by Sami. Nawah's voice over a 92 BPM beat with a bass line that commands space. Available on all platforms.

## LIO-K — Verre Cassé

Raw drill, tracked in two sessions. What we love: the economy of the arrangements. No unnecessary layers — just the beat, the voice, and the 808 doing the work.

## MARA — Néon

We didn't produce this one. But we're here to support the local scene. MARA drops a pop single with outside production and a hook that stayed in our heads for a week. Worth your ears.
